Creamy Chicken Cordon Bleu Casserole

This food is very tasty and doesn't require a lot of time!

  • Prep time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 45mins
  • Total time: 55mins

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Chicken(cubed and cooked)
  • Ham(cubed and cooked)
  • Soup
  • Sour cream and milk(2% milk)
  • Butter
  • Breab crumbs

Steps:

  1. Boil and drain the noodles, then transfer to a prepared casserole dish.
  2. Top the noodles with chicken, ham, and Swiss cheese.
  3. Combine the soup, milk, and sour cream in a bowl, then spoon over the noodle mixture.
  4. Make the topping and sprinkle it over the casserole.
  5. Bake until the casserole is bubbling and lightly browned.
